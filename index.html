How We Work

Our Design Process

A streamlined, transparent process from first conversation to final handover.

  1. 01

    Consultation

    We begin with a free consultation to understand your needs, style, and budget.

  2. 02

    Site Visit

    Our team visits your space to take measurements and assess site conditions.

  3. 03

    Planning

    We develop a detailed design brief, layout plan, and project timeline.

  4. 04

    3D Design

    Photorealistic 3D renders bring your future space to life before execution begins.

  5. 05

    Material Selection

    You choose from curated finishes, fabrics, and materials suited to your design.

  6. 06

    Execution

    Our skilled craftsmen bring the design to life with precision and quality control.

  7. 07

    Final Handover

    A final walkthrough and quality check before we hand over your beautifully finished space.
